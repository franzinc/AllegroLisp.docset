urn-nid

Arguments: urn-uri

URN's are a subset of URI's. URN objects are members of the urn class. The Namespace ID determines the syntactic interpretation of the Namespace Specific String of an URN. Thus: 

cl-user(7): (setq urn (net.uri:parse-uri "urn:foo:10"))
#<uri urn:foo:10>
cl-user(8): (net.uri:urn-nid urn)
"foo"
cl-user(9): (net.uri:urn-nss urn)
"10"
cl-user(10):

See also uri.htm for general information on Universal Resource Indicator support in Allegro CL.
